About



Early Life

Eldis Salihovic is a Bosnian American who was born in Tuzla, Bosnia and Herzegovina in 1992. When he was three years old, him and his family fled their home and migrated to the United States because of a war. The six republics of Yugoslavia collapsed causing the destruction of villages, towns, families, and cities. Eldis has spent most of his life in the twin cities area of Minnesota learning to speak Bosnian, English, and other languages to communicate with his family and people at school.


Career

Eldis’s first job was at Cub Foods at the age of sixteen where his mother and father worked. He had the opportunity to gain customer service skills at Cub Foods. At the age of eighteen, he obtained a position as a teller at TCF bank where he elevated his customer service skills, gained banking and sales experience, learned new procedures and learned how to reconcile cash, coupons, and other transactions. After TCF bank, Eldis explored other opportunities with US Bank and Wells Fargo where he had the opportunity to move up and progress into Personal Banker and Supervisor roles. He had the opportunity to train and develop other employees, resolve complex customer issues, facilitate team meetings, oversee transactions, manage the team and inventory, and advise customers on banking products and services for their needs. After US Bank and Wells Fargo, Eldis was interested in serving for this country, so he joined the United States Navy. Boot camp had to be one of the toughest experiences he has been through mentally and physically, but he made it after two months and it was one of his greatest accomplishments. After serving in the military, Eldis obtained a position as a Senior Security Specialist for Target Corporation where he had the opportunity to manage security operations and serve as a resource to leaders, team members, and partners as a subject matter expert. During his time at Target Corporation, Eldis picked up a second position part time as a Specialist at Apple where he had the opportunity to serve customers by uncovering their needs, offering meaningful solutions, and helping them set up their new products. Currently, Eldis works at Bowlero as an Operations manager where he manages the center from food, beverage, entertainment, employees to the property and equipment. He consistently delivers exceptional service, receives positive feedback, and actively addresses and resolves complex guest complaints and issues. He maximizes productivity and leads his team effectively by establishing and maintaining strong relationships, trust, respect, and compassion. He consistently checks in with others to support and assist, delegates tasks, and ensure others’ voices are heard.


Values

Eldis chose to pursue a career in his industry because he is passionate about helping others and expanding his knowledge. This passion stems from his personal experiences including the struggles he and his family endured. He over analyzes and approaches every scenario strategically to ensure he does not miss the intricate details. He is a people person and values authenticity, connection, and challenge. Therefore, he aims to be genuine, mindfully present, engaged, open-minded, and compassionate. He also strives to keep challenging himself and others to learn, grow, and improve.


Aspirations

His aspirations personally include getting married, having children, staying active, and being healthy. Professionally, he aspires to be a director or owner of a company in the future.


Hobbies

Eldis’s hobbies include spending time with family, weightlifting, soccer, watching movies, vacationing, and eating. His favorite foods are sushi, pho, and Bosnian dishes.
